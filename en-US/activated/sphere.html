Congratulations, Sphere is now activated in Firefox!

Services like Sphere help you to keep up with friends, see the stories people are talking about and more. Keep on reading to find out more about how these features work.

Get the most out of your Sphere sidebar

Starting with Firefox 30, You can manage your sidebars using the sidebar toolbarbutton. You can find the sidebar toolbar button in the customization palette.

You can easily change the service in your sidebar, or hide the sidebar, from the sidebar itself. You can also use the View->Toolbars menu in Firefox.

Change Settings from the Add-on Manager

You can easily manage your services, disabling them temporarily or removing them, by viewing the Add-ons Manager in Firefox.
